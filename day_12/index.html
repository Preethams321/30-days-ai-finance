About MacroPulse

MacroPulse is an India-first economic calendar that surfaces every meaningful macro event that moves Indian markets, explained in plain English for retail investors. Updated dynamically — no stale hardcoded dates.

Data sources & methodology

  • 🏦 RBI MPC dates: Verified from official RBI announcement dated March 23, 2026. FY27 schedule.
  • 📊 Recurring data releases: Computed dynamically in JavaScript from today's date, based on typical MOSPI, RBI, and SIAM release windows (12th–15th of month etc.).
  • 📈 F&O Expiry: Nifty 50 weekly (Tuesday), Bank Nifty monthly (last Tuesday of month), Sensex weekly (Thursday). Per SEBI circular effective Sep 1, 2025.
  • 🌐 Global events: Approximate cadence. Exact FOMC dates from federalreserve.gov; NFP = first Friday of month; US CPI = mid-month; China PMI = 1st of month.
  • 🏛️ Union Budget / Economic Survey: Annual. Feb 1 and Jan 31 respectively.
