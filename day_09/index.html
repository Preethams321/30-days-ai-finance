    Key Signals
    Exact phrases from RBI documents — decoded
    📖 How to Read These
    Central banks communicate in carefully chosen language. The difference between “vigilant” and “watchful”, or “prudent to wait” versus “monitoring closely”, tells you how close the RBI is to its next move. Read the plain English translation under each signal.
    06
    Sector Impact
    How this MPC decision affects key sectors
    📖 How to Use This
    Each sector score (0–100) reflects how positive or negative this MPC outcome is. Green (above 60) means tailwinds. Amber (40–60) is mixed. Red (below 40) means headwinds. These are relative to this meeting’s decision — not absolute business quality assessments.
    07
    Capital Flow Measures
    Policy actions announced alongside the rate decision
    📖 Why Capital Flows Matter
    The RBI doesn’t just set rates — it also manages India’s balance of payments. When FPI outflows are high, RBI takes steps to attract dollar inflows through NRI deposits, bond market access, and borrowing incentives. These measures show RBI actively managing external account pressures.
